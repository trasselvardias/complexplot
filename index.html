Complex Function Visualizer

Correct-aspect domain coloring, Riemann sphere, and flat sphere projections for teaching complex analysis.
Examples: z^2, 1/z, sin(z), exp(z)/(z^3-1), z^5-1, sech(z)

Mouse controls
Plane: drag to pan, wheel to zoom. The plane is rendered with equal mathematical x/y scale when “equal x/y scale” is on.

Sphere/projection: drag to rotate the sphere coordinates, wheel to zoom. Projection modes flatten the Riemann sphere onto the canvas while preserving the same domain coloring.
